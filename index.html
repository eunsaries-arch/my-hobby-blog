My Favourite Hobbies

Hello! Welcome to my blog, and nice to meet you guys ^^ I just want to share a few things I enjoy doing during my free time, especially when I feel upset or stressed. Since I’m an introvert, I actually enjoy spending time by myself and getting lost in my own little world. Watching TV shows, writing, and listening to music are my favorite ways to relax and express myself

Watching Drama

First of all, I spend a lot of my time watching TV shows, especially dramas. I’m a huge fan of K-dramas! I enjoy many different genres, but thriller K-dramas are definitely my favorite. I love the suspense and unexpected plot twists—they always keep me hooked. One of my all-time favorite K-dramas is Mouse, starring Lee Seung Gi. Whenever someone asks for a thriller K-drama recommendation, this is always the first one I suggest.
